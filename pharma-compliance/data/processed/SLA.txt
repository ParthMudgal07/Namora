STATE LICENSING AUTHORITY (SLA) – COMPLIANCE
FRAMEWORK
1. Legal Framework
Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940; Drugs and Cosmetics Rules, 1945; Medical Devices Rules, 2017;
New Drugs and Clinical Trials Rules, 2019; Cosmetics Rules, 2020.
2. Role of SLA
Grant licenses, conduct inspections, enforce compliance, monitor drug quality.
3. Licensing
Manufacturing License: GMP compliance, qualified staff, approved premises.
Sale License: Retail and Wholesale with proper storage and pharmacist.
Loan License: Manufacture at another licensed facility.
4. Inspection & Enforcement
Pre-licensing, routine, surprise, and complaint-based inspections.
Actions: suspension, cancellation, seizure, prosecution.
5. GMP (Schedule M)
Quality systems, sanitation, equipment validation, documentation, batch records.
6. Documentation
BMR, BPR, distribution records, complaint records, recall procedures.
7. Labeling
Drug name, batch number, expiry, license number, storage, schedule classification.
8. Schedule Drugs

--- PAGE 2 ---

Schedule H/H1/X: prescription-only, strict record keeping.
9. Pharmacovigilance
Adverse drug reaction monitoring and reporting.
10. Medical Devices
Classification A-D, ISO 13485, licensing, surveillance.
11. Cosmetics
Manufacturing license, safe ingredients, labeling compliance.
12. Penalties
Fines, imprisonment, license cancellation, recalls.
13. Compliance Checklist
Valid license, GMP compliance, documentation, labeling, staff, inspection readiness.
